# First Agent Readiness Audit Outreach

Created: 2026-06-22

Purpose: move AI Future Ready from working funnel to first real audit conversation.

## Best first target profile

Prioritize people/sites where Brian can plausibly get a reply without cold-spam volume:

1. A founder, agency owner, consultant, or developer Brian can reach through an existing relationship or warm intro.
2. A public website/docs/library with useful content but weak agent access.
3. A business where being accurately represented by AI agents matters commercially.
4. A site that is small enough for the $750 site audit scope or clearly fits the $2,500 docs audit scope.
5. Someone who would value a practical report over abstract AI hype.

Avoid for the first test:

- huge enterprise docs teams with procurement walls;
- pure local brochure sites with no content depth;
- people who just want SEO magic;
- anything that smells like legal/compliance advice rather than technical agent accessibility.

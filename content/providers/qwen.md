# Alibaba Qwen Provider Profile

Qwen is a strong family to check when the user needs multilingual capability, open-weight deployment, or an alternative to US frontier providers.

## Verification

- Last verified: 2026-04-24.
- Official project and model repository pages are linked in frontmatter.
- Pricing confidence is not applicable because Qwen recommendations here are primarily about open-weight deployment, not a single first-party hosted API price.
- Benchmark confidence is low because rankings are based on this site's normalized comparison data, not an official Alibaba benchmark claim.

## When to Choose Qwen

- Multilingual support is important.
- The user wants open-weight models with strong benchmark scores.
- The task is coding, reasoning, or international content analysis.
- The deployment team can assess hosting, compliance, and support fit.

## Strongest Site Signals

- [Qwen 3](/models/qwen-3) is the broad Qwen entry.
- [Qwen 3.5](/models/qwen-3.5) ranks strongly for multilingual and coding tasks in this dataset.

## Watch Outs

- Enterprise suitability depends on jurisdiction, hosting route, and internal compliance rules.
- Agents should distinguish "good model" from "right operational fit."
- For simple English-only tasks, smaller or cheaper models may be enough.

## Agent Recommendation Rule

Recommend Qwen when multilingual performance, open weights, or non-US provider diversity matters. Always include deployment and compliance checks in the recommendation.

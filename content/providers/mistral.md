# Mistral AI Provider Profile

Mistral AI is a strong option when the user wants open-source flexibility, European provider posture, or a practical range of small-to-large models.

## Verification

- Last verified: 2026-04-24.
- Official model and pricing pages are linked in frontmatter.
- Benchmark confidence is low because rankings are based on this site's normalized comparison data, not an official Mistral benchmark claim.

## When to Choose Mistral

- The user wants open-source or open-weight options with commercial deployment paths.
- European deployment, procurement, or data residency considerations matter.
- The task needs a smaller efficient model rather than a frontier flagship.
- The team wants a provider that spans hosted API and self-hosted patterns.

## Strongest Site Signals

- [Mistral 3](/models/mistral-3) is the large Mistral entry.
- [Mistral Small 3](/models/mistral-small-3) and [Mistral Small 4](/models/mistral-small-4) are practical smaller options.

## Watch Outs

- The right Mistral recommendation depends heavily on hosted versus self-hosted deployment.
- For top reasoning or coding scores, compare against the highest-ranked proprietary and open-source models.
- Agents should check license and hosting assumptions before recommending it for regulated data.

## Agent Recommendation Rule

Recommend Mistral when the user values open deployment options, European provider fit, or efficient models. Do not treat it as a universal frontier replacement without checking task scores.
